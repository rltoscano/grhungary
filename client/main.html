currency

Even though Hungary is a member of the European Union, the “forint” remains the official currency in Hungary. As of Jan 25 2013, the exchange rate is:

1 USD = 220.95 Forint

weather

Summers in Hungary are very hot and dry and daily temperatures in July often reach above 86 Fahrenheit. Nighttime temperatures are cooler, though still warm. Sandals and shorts are comfortable attire both day and night.

Kefalonia is the most westerly island of Greece lies in the Ionian Sea, in the channel leading into the Gulf of Patras. With its circa 780 square kilometres, the island is the largest of the Eptanissos (the Seven Islands). Kefalonia is characterized by the creative forces of nature and three thousand years of cultural history.
