travel

airfare

Plane ticket prices to Budapest, Hungary for the summer season start around $1100 and go up from there. You can use any travel websites to book your flights. For example:

  • www.bing.com/travel
  • www.kayak.com
  • www.orbitz.com
  • and many others.
weather

The weather in Hungary is very warm during the summer. Temperatures range between 80-100 Fahrenheit.

  1. Gellért-hegy/Gellért Hill, Libegő/Budapest Chairlift, Citadella/Citadel with gorgeous views of the city.
  2. Budai Vár/ Buda Castle (there are several museums inside of the castle)

    Halászbástya/Fisherman’s Bastion

    Mátyás-templom/Matthias Church
  3. Parlament / The House of Parliament
  4. Margitsziget/Margaret Island
  5. Andrássy út/Andrássy Avenue

    Háza/House of Terror

    Magyar Állami Operaház/Hungarian State Opera House
  6. Hősök tere/Heroes’ Square

  7. Városliget/City Park
  8. Széchenyi Gyógyfürdő és Uszoda/Szechenyi Bath and Spa Budapest: http://www.szechenyibath.com
