welcome

Welcome to our wedding website! We are delighted to be able to share the celebration of our marriage with you. For all of you who will be attending, all of the information you could ever want to know about us and our wedding will be available for you here. For those of you who will not be able to join us, we thought this would be a great way for us to share all of our experiences with you. In the following pages, you will find details about how we met, as well as information about the wedding and the reception.

After three wonderful years of having been married in a small ceremony at the San Francisco City Hall on March 1st, 2010, we are looking forward to our dream wedding with our families and friends in Ráckeve, Hungary. We have chosen to get married in the Savoyai Mansion Hotel, a beautiful landmark just six miles away from Gyöngyi’s hometown. It's absolutely beautiful there and we know you will love it.

Our goal is to create an experience that allows you to discover each others’ cultures through excellent food, drinks, and fun times. We think that the celebration of our union provides the perfect opportunity for our families and friends to meet and get to know one another. We cannot wait to see all of you together.

For those of you who will be coming in from out of town, we have provided you with many wonderful suggestions of places to stay. Hopefully some of you will decide to make a vacation out of this trip to Hungary and extend your stay to a full week or more. If you have the option, we encourage you to visit some of the nearby countries, as many of Europe’s amazing cities are only a short train or plane ride away from Budapest.

We are looking forward to seeing everyone in just a few months. Thank you to all of our friends and family for supporting us and loving us over the years.

Love,
Gyöngyi and Robert

travel

airfare

Plane ticket prices and travel lengths from the U.S. west coast to Budapest, Hungary depend on the number of stopovers you have. For the summer season, air travel costs average around $1300 round trip and the duration of the flight around 16 hours. You can use any travel websites to book your flights. Here are a few examples:

  • Bing Travel
  • Kayak
  • Orbitz
  • eDreams

Please plan on arriving to Hungary no later than Friday, in case there are any delays in your flight schedule. If you would like to allow yourself enough time to adjust to the time difference and perhaps visit some sightseeing destinations, you might want to arrive a few days earlier.

accommodation

Since the wedding ceremony and the following reception are going to be at the Savoyai Mansion Hotel, we recommend that you book your rooms there. We have asked the hotel to set aside 19 rooms for our guests to reserve for July 26-28. The following rooms have been set aside:

  • 4 single beds
  • 9 double beds
  • 2 triple beds
  • 4 four beds

Please mention in your reservation that your stay is for the Gózon-Toscano wedding. Prices range from $40 per night to $84. If you would like to stay at the hotel longer or shorter than the above mentioned three nights, you can do that as well. Additional hotels in Ráckeve are:

  • Hotel Termálkristály Aqualand
  • Kék Duna Wellness Hotel

directions to venue

If you plan to go straight to the location of the wedding from Ferenc Liszt International Airport, we will arrange transportation for you. If you arrive to Hungary a few days earlier and aim to visit Budapest, you can catch a bus or a train at the airport that will take you to the city centre. currency

Even though Hungary is a member of the European Union, the “forint” remains the official currency in Hungary. As of Jan 25 2013, the exchange rate is:

1 USD = 220.95 Forint

weather

Summers in Hungary are very hot and dry and daily temperatures in July often reach above 86 Fahrenheit. Nighttime temperatures are cooler, though still warm. Sandals and shorts are comfortable attire both day and night.

Kefalonia is the most westerly island of Greece lies in the Ionian Sea, in the channel leading into the Gulf of Patras. With its circa 780 square kilometres, the island is the largest of the Eptanissos (the Seven Islands). Turquoise seas, lush green vegetation, wild and sheer mountain scenery and a fascinating variety of geological profiles have given Kefalonia a special atmosphere and beauty. Kefalonia is characterized by the creative forces of nature and three thousand years of cultural history.
